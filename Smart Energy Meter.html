SMART ENERGY METER

Objective:

Smart Energy Meter, a system that monitors electricity usage in real time. In industries, factories, and even large buildings, it is very important to know how much power is being used at every moment. Without proper monitoring, a lot of electricity gets wasted. Our project helps track voltage, current, and total power consumption instantly on both a mobile phone and a panel. This allows industries to manage energy efficiently and reduce daily electricity costs.”
